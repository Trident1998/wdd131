Site Plan: Bukovel Chronicles

WDD 131 Project

Site Name

Bukovel Chronicles

I chose "Bukovel Chronicles" because a chronicle is a historical record of events. This name fits the dual purpose of the site: documenting the timeless visual beauty of the resort (Gallery) and recording the personal memories of the people who visit (Stories).

Optional domain: bukovel-chronicles.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a living archive for the Bukovel resort. It goes beyond simple tourist information by focusing on immersive storytelling. It allows prospective travelers to see the resort through high-quality photography and allows past visitors to preserve their memories by submitting personal stories about their time in the Carpathians.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "I am planning a photography trip to the Carpathians. I want to see a gallery of high-quality images filtered by season (Winter vs. Summer) to decide which equipment to bring and what time of year offers the best views."
  • Scenario 2: "I have a funny story about learning to ski on the beginner slopes. I want to find a place where I can write it down and share it with other travel enthusiasts."

Branding

Color Schema (WCAG AAA Compliant)

To meet the strict WCAG AAA accessibility standards (7:1 contrast ratio), this palette uses high-contrast, deep earth tones inspired by the dark winter forests and treated timber of Bukovel.
